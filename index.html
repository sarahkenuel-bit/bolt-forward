Bolt Forward Facilitator Setup Guide
Step 1 — Host the simulation
Deploy to Netlify (free, 2 minutes)
1. Download this file — click the download icon in the Claude artifact panel.
2. Go to netlify.com and create a free account.
3. From your Netlify dashboard, find the drag and drop deploy box and drag the file in.
4. Copy the URL Netlify gives you and share with all teams before the session.
Step 2 — On the day setup
Each team needs one laptop. All teams open the same Netlify URL.

You (facilitator) open the same URL and click "Facilitator view →" — keep this on the projector throughout.

Recommended: 6 teams of 5 people. One person per team drives the laptop.
Step 3 — Running each year
Teams make their decisions and press "Submit Scores →" at the end of each year. They cannot proceed until submitted.

You hit "↺ Refresh" on the facilitator leaderboard to pull in all scores, show on projector, and facilitate discussion before releasing teams to the next year.

After Year 3 the facilitator view shows the podium automatically.
Step 4 — The debrief
The Mirror Question — Where did vertical and enterprise interest diverge — and which one won?
The System Question — Where is the connected system already running in your real organisation?
The Commitment — One behaviour change. Yours. In the next 30 days.
